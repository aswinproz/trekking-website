The Ultimate Khumbu Circuit Adventure

The Everest Three Passes Trek is the most comprehensive way to experience Nepal's legendary Khumbu region. This extraordinary 21-day journey takes you across three high mountain passes, each offering breathtaking panoramic views of the world's highest peaks including Mount Everest (8,848m), Lhotse, Makalu, and Cho Oyu.

Unlike the standard Everest Base Camp trek, this route creates a complete circuit that ventures into every corner of the Khumbu. You'll stand at the foot of Everest at Base Camp, witness sunrise from Kalapathar at 5,545m, explore the sacred turquoise Gokyo Lakes, and cross three passes exceeding 5,300 meters in altitude.

This trek represents the pinnacle of Himalayan trekking - a true adventure that tests your physical limits while rewarding you with memories that will last a lifetime.

Trip Highlights

  • Cross three high mountain passes: Renjo La, Cho La, and Kongma La
  • Stand at Everest Base Camp (5,364m) - the foot of the world's highest peak
  • Experience breathtaking sunrise at Kalapathar (5,545m)
  • Explore all five sacred Gokyo Lakes
  • Hike to Gokyo Ri for stunning 360° Himalayan views
  • Visit iconic Sherpa villages including Namche Bazaar and Dingboche
  • Experience rich Sherpa culture and ancient Buddhist monasteries
  • Complete circuit of the Khumbu region
  • Scenic flights to and from Lukla
  • Expert guided experience with proper acclimatization
Detailed Itinerary

21-Day Adventure Awaits

A carefully planned route ensuring proper acclimatization

1 Arrival in Kathmandu (1,300m)

+

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport. Our representative welcomes you with traditional namaste. Transfer to hotel, welcome dinner with cultural show, and trek briefing. Overnight in Kathmandu.

2 Fly to Lukla (2,840m) & Trek to Phakding (2,610m)

+

Early morning scenic flight to Lukla. Begin trek to Phakding through pine forests and across suspension bridges. Overnight in Phakding.

3 Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m)

+

Trek to the gateway of Everest - Namche Bazaar. First views of Mount Everest. Overnight in Namche.

4 Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

+

Explore Sherpa Museum, visit Everest View Hotel for close-up mountain views. Acclimatization rest day.

5 Trek to Thame (3,800m)

+

Trek to traditional Sherpa village of Thame. Visit famous Thame Monastery. Overnight in Thame.

6 Cross Renjo La (5,340m) to Gokyo (4,790m)

+

First high pass crossing - Renjo La. Spectacular panoramic views. Descend to sacred Gokyo Valley. Overnight in Gokyo.

7 Explore Gokyo Valley

+

Hike to Gokyo Ri (5,360m) for breathtaking sunrise over Everest. Explore all five Gokyo Lakes.

8 Trek to Dzongla (4,830m)

+

Trek towards second pass via Scoundrels Viewpoint. Magnificent views of Cholatse and Taboche. Overnight in Dzongla.

9 Cross Cho La (5,420m) to Lobuche (4,940m)

+

Second high pass crossing - Cho La. Rugged glacial pass with magnificent views. Descend to Lobuche.

10 Trek to Everest Base Camp (5,364m) & Return to Gorakshep

+

Stand at Everest Base Camp! Walk through Khumbu Icefall area. Return to Gorakshep for overnight.

11 Sunrise at Kalapathar (5,545m) & Trek to Dzongla

+

Iconic Kalapathar sunrise. Most spectacular close-up view of Everest's north face. Descend to Dzongla.

12 Rest Day in Dzongla

+

Rest and acclimatization day before final pass. Explore area or visit Buddhist monastery.

13 Cross Kongma La (5,535m) to Chhungkook (4,730m)

+

Final and highest pass - Kongma La at 5,535m. Stunning views of Makalu and Island Peak. Descend to Chhungkook.

14 Explore Chhungkook Valley

+

Optional hike to Island Peak Base Camp or Chhungkook Ri viewpoint. Enjoy Himalayan landscapes.

15 Return Trek via Dingboche

+

Return journey through Imja Valley to Dingboche. Views of Island Peak. Overnight in Dingboche.

16 Trek to Namche Bazaar

+

Descend through Tengboche and Phunki Tenga back to Namche Bazaar. Celebrate your achievement!

17 Trek to Lukla

+

Final day on the trail. Descend through pine forests to Lukla. Celebrate completion of epic journey.

18 Buffer Day for Weather

+

Reserved day for flight delays due to weather. Explore Lukla or rest.

19 Fly back to Kathmandu

+

Return flight to Kathmandu. Free time for shopping. Farewell dinner.

20 Free Day in Kathmandu

+

Full day in Kathmandu. Optional sightseeing to Pashupatinath, Boudhanath, or Bhaktapur.

21 Departure

+

Transfer to airport for departure. Bid farewell with incredible memories of your Himalayan adventure!
