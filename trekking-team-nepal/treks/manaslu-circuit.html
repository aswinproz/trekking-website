An Epic Adventure Around Manaslu

The Manaslu Circuit Trek is one of Nepal's most spectacular and challenging treks, circumnavigating Manaslu (8,163m), the eighth highest mountain in the world. This 18-day adventure takes you through the remote Manaslu Conservation Area, across the dramatic Larkya La Pass at 5,136m, and into ancient Buddhist villages where Tibetan culture thrives.

Unlike the more crowded Everest and Annapurna regions, the Manaslu Circuit offers a pristine Himalayan experience with fewer tourists, making it perfect for those seeking adventure away from the crowds. The trek combines breathtaking mountain scenery, diverse ecosystems, rich cultural heritage, and the satisfaction of crossing one of the highest passes in Nepal.

Trip Highlights

  • Cross the legendary Larkya La Pass at 5,136m
  • Visit the magnificent Manaslu Base Camp
  • Explore ancient Buddhist villages and monasteries
  • Experience authentic Tibetan culture
  • Witness stunning Himalayan sunrises and sunsets
  • Trek through pristine rhododendron and bamboo forests
  • See diverse wildlife in Manaslu Conservation Area

Detailed Itinerary

18-Day Manaslu Circuit Trek

A complete day-by-day breakdown of your Himalayan adventure

1 Arrival in Kathmandu

+

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu where our representative will welcome you with a traditional Nepali greeting. Transfer to your hotel and enjoy some rest after your journey. In the evening, join us for a welcome dinner featuring traditional Nepali cuisine and a cultural dance show. Your trek guide will provide a comprehensive briefing about the upcoming adventure and check your equipment.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu | Meals: Dinner

2 Drive to Soti Khola (700m)

+

After an early breakfast, embark on a scenic 7-8 hour drive to Soti Khola, the starting point of your trek. The journey takes you through the Trishuli river valley, passing through beautiful villages, terraced fields, and lush forests. Enjoy the changing landscapes as you travel from the Kathmandu Valley to the mountainous region. Arrive at Soti Khola in the late afternoon and settle into your teahouse.

Overnight: Teahouse at Soti Khola | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

3 Trek to Machha Khola (900m)

+

Your first day of trekking begins with a walk through beautiful forests and rural villages. Follow the Budhi Gandaki River upstream, crossing several suspension bridges. The trail passes through Soti Khola and climbs through a beautiful bamboo forest before descending to Machha Khola (Fish River), named after the fish in its waters. Today's hike takes approximately 5-6 hours.

Overnight: Teahouse at Machha Khola | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

4 Trek to Jagat (1,340m)

+

Continue your trek along the Budhi Gandaki River, passing through remote villages and terraced fields. Enter the Manaslu Conservation Area, where you need to register your permits. The trail features stone villages with traditional architecture, and you'll see beautiful waterfalls along the way. Arrive in Jagat, a large village that serves as a major checkpoint, after 5-6 hours of trekking.

Overnight: Teahouse at Jagat | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

5 Trek to Deng (1,860m)

+

Leave Jagat and continue through the valley, crossing several suspension bridges. The trail becomes more remote as you ascend through forests and small villages. You'll pass through Pewa and Lokpa before reaching Deng, a small village with traditional houses. Today involves 5-6, gaining altitude gradually as hours of trekking you enter the heart of the Manaslu region.

Overnight: Teahouse at Deng | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

6 Trek to Namrung (2,630m)

+

A challenging but rewarding day as you climb higher into the Himalayas. The trail passes through forests and crosses the Budhi Gandaki River multiple times. As you gain altitude, the landscape changes, and you'll catch your first glimpses of the Manaslu massif and other peaks. Arrive in Namrung, a beautiful village with stunning mountain views, after 5-6 hours of trekking.

Overnight: Teahouse at Namrung | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

7 Trek to Sama Gaon (3,530m) - Acclimatization

+

Continue ascending through alpine terrain to reach Sama Gaon, the largest village in the Manaslu region. This is an important acclimatization stop before heading to higher altitudes. Take it easy today and explore the beautiful village with its Buddhist monastery and traditional houses. Enjoy the stunning views of Manaslu and surrounding peaks. Today's trek takes 5-6 hours.

Overnight: Teahouse at Sama Gaon | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

8 Explore Sama Gaon - Hike to Manaslu Base Camp

+

Today is a highlight of the trek as you hike to Manaslu Base Camp (4,900m). The trail follows the glacier moraine with increasingly impressive views of Manaslu's massive icefall and surrounding peaks. At base camp, you'll stand at the foot of the eighth highest mountain in the world. Return to Sama Gaon for overnight stay. This is a challenging 6-7 hour day but absolutely unforgettable.

Overnight: Teahouse at Sama Gaon | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

9 Trek to Samdo (3,875m)

+

Descend from Sama Gaon and cross the Budhi Gandaki River to reach Samdo, a beautiful village near the Tibetan border. Samdo offers spectacular views of the Manaslu range and is known for its traditional Tibetan-style houses. This relatively short 4-5 hour trek allows for proper acclimatization while bringing you closer to the high mountain passes.

Overnight: Teahouse at Samdo | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

10 Trek to Dharmasala (4,460m)

+

A short but important trekking day as you ascend to Dharmasala (also known as Larkya Phedi), the last settlement before crossing the Larkya La Pass. The trail is rocky and alpine, with stunning views of the surrounding peaks. At Dharmasala, you'll rest and prepare for the challenging pass crossing tomorrow. This is 4-5 hours of trekking at high altitude.

Overnight: Teahouse at Dharmasala | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

11 Cross Larkya La Pass (5,136m) to Bimthang (3,720m)

+

This is the most challenging and rewarding day of the trek. Start very early (around 4 AM) to cross the Larkya La Pass at 5,136m, the highest point of the trek. The climb is steep but manageable, and the views from the top - panorama of Manaslu, Himlung, Cheo, and other peaks - are absolutely breathtaking. Descend to Bimthang through alpine meadows, a long but unforgettable day of 7-8 hours.

Overnight: Teahouse at Bimthang | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

12 Trek to Tilije (2,300m)

+

Descend from Bimthang through beautiful alpine meadows and forests. The trail passes through several small villages and crosses the Dudh Khola river. As you descend, the climate becomes warmer and the vegetation changes. Arrive in Tilije, a charming village known for its stone houses and terraced fields. Today's trek takes 5-6 hours.

Overnight: Teahouse at Tilije | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

13 Trek to Chamje (1,410m)

+

Continue descending along the Marshyangdi River valley, passing through forests and traditional villages. The trail is relatively easier today as you continue to lose altitude. Arrive in Chamje, a village located by the river, after 5-6 hours of trekking. This area marks the transition from the Manaslu region to the more familiar Annapurna region.

Overnight: Teahouse at Chamje | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

14 Drive to Besisahar - Drive to Kathmandu

+

After 14 days of trekking, enjoy a well-deserved rest as you drive back to Kathmandu. The drive takes approximately 8-9 hours, following the Marshyangdi River and then the Trishuli River valley back to the capital. Arrive in Kathmandu in the evening and transfer to your hotel. Celebrate the completion of your epic Manaslu Circuit adventure with a farewell dinner.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu | Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

15-17 Buffer Days

+

These buffer days are built into the itinerary to account for potential delays due to weather, altitude sickness, or other unforeseen circumstances. If your trek goes according to plan, these days can be used for additional exploration in Kathmandu, shopping for souvenirs, or simply relaxing. Your guide will coordinate with you on how to best utilize these days.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu | Meals: Breakfast (Days 15-16)

18 Departure

+

After an unforgettable adventure on the Manaslu Circuit, it's time to depart. Our representative will transfer you to Tribhuvan International Airport for your return flight. Take with you memories of breathtaking mountain vistas, ancient Buddhist villages, and the accomplishment of completing one of Nepal's greatest treks. We look forward to welcoming you back for more Himalayan adventures!

Meals: Breakfast
