Journey to the Last Forbidden Kingdom

The Upper Dolpo Trek offers an extraordinary journey through one of Nepal's most remote and culturally rich regions. Often referred to as the "Last Forbidden Kingdom," Upper Dolpo remained isolated from the outside world for centuries, preserving a unique Tibetan Buddhist culture that dates back over a thousand years.

This 22-day expedition takes you through pristine landscapes, ancient monasteries, and traditional villages where time seems to stand still. The highlight of the trek is the stunning Phoksundo Lake, with its turquoise waters that appear almost magical against the surrounding brown hills. You'll also visit the legendary Shey Monastery, one of the oldest in Nepal, and cross high mountain passes offering breathtaking panoramic views of the Dhaulagiri range.

Unlike the more popular trekking routes in Nepal, Upper Dolpo remains largely untouched by modernization, offering a truly authentic wilderness experience. The region's restricted access has helped preserve its natural beauty and cultural heritage, making this trek a unique opportunity to explore one of the Himalayas' best-kept secrets.

Trek Highlights

  • Phoksundo Lake - The stunning turquoise lake at 3,600m with remarkable color changes
  • Shey Monastery - One of the oldest and most sacred monasteries in Nepal
  • Ancient Tibetan Culture - Experience traditions preserved for over a thousand years
  • Shey Phoksundo National Park - Home to blue sheep, snow leopards, and diverse wildlife
  • High Mountain Passes - Cross passes with breathtaking Himalayan views
  • Ringmo Village - Traditional Tibetan village with ancient chortens and prayer walls
  • Remote Wilderness - Pristine landscapes largely untouched by modernization
  • Unique Wildlife - Spot rare Himalayan wildlife in their natural habitat
Day by Day

Detailed Itinerary

1Arrival in Kathmandu

+

Welcome to Nepal! Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with a traditional Nepali cultural show. Later, receive a trek briefing and prepare all necessary permits. Overnight in Kathmandu.

2Fly to Nepalgunj

+

Take an early morning flight from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj (approximately 1 hour). Nepalgunj is a bustling border town in the Terai region, close to the Indian border. Explore the local market and prepare for the next day's flight to Juphal. Overnight in Nepalgunj.

3Fly to Juphal (2,400m)

+

Morning flight from Nepalgunj to Juphal, the gateway to Dolpo region (approximately 20 minutes). The flight offers spectacular views of the Himalayas. Upon arrival, meet your trekking crew and begin the adventure. Overnight in Juphal.

4-5Trek to Dunai (2,850m)

+

Begin your trek from Juphal, following the Thuli Bheri River through lush forests and gradual ascents. Day 4 involves 5-6 hours of trekking to reach Dunai, the administrative center of Dolpa District. Day 5 is a rest and exploration day in Dunai - visit the local monastery, explore the village, and prepare for the journey to Phoksundo Lake. Acclimatization is key for the days ahead.

6-7Trek to Phoksundo Lake (3,600m)

+

Day 6: A challenging 6-7 hour trek takes you dramatically down into the Phoksundo valley. The trail descends through pine forests before emerging to reveal the spectacular Phoksundo Lake - a stunning body of turquoise water surrounded by vertical cliffs. Day 7: Full day to explore this magical lake. Visit the nearby waterfall, photography the remarkable color changes of the lake throughout the day, and soak in the serene atmosphere of one of Nepal's most beautiful lakes.

8-9Trek to Ringmo (3,700m)

+

Day 8: Trek for 5-6 hours to the traditional village of Ringmo, known for its ancient Buddhist chortens and mani walls. The village offers stunning views and a glimpse into traditional Dolpo culture. Day 9: Rest and acclimatization day in Ringmo. Explore the local temples, interact with friendly villagers, and enjoy the panoramic mountain views. This is an important rest day before ascending to higher altitudes.

10-12Shey Phoksundo National Park

+

Days 10-12: Enter the Shey Phoksundo National Park, Nepal's largest national park. Trek through pristine forests, remote settlements, and dramatic mountain terrain. The park is home to diverse wildlife including blue sheep, snow leopards, and over 200 species of birds. Each day involves 5-7 hours of trekking, gradually ascending through the park as you make your way toward Upper Dolpo. These days offer spectacular mountain views and opportunities to spot wildlife.

13-15Cross Passes to Upper Dolpo

+

Days 13-15: The most challenging section of the trek as you cross high mountain passes exceeding 4,500m. These days involve steep ascents and descents on rocky terrain. The effort is rewarded with spectacular panoramic views of the Dhaulagiri range and surrounding peaks. Trek deeper into the remote Upper Dolpo region, passing through ancient villages where traditional ways of life continue unchanged for centuries.

16-18Explore Upper Dolpo

+

Days 16-18: Three full days to explore the heart of Upper Dolpo. Visit remote villages where ancient Tibetan traditions are preserved, explore sacred monasteries, and interact with local communities. This is the highlight of the trek - experiencing a culture that remains largely untouched by the modern world. Enjoy photography opportunities, cultural exchanges, and the profound silence of this remote wilderness.

19-21Return to Juphal

+

Begin the return journey, retracing your steps through the national park and back toward Juphal. Days 19-20 involve 6-7 hours of trekking each day, descending through familiar landscapes with new appreciation. Day 21: Final day of trekking, reaching the Juphal area for your flight the next morning. Celebrate the completion of this extraordinary journey with your trekking crew.

22Departure

+

Morning flight from Juphal to Nepalgunj, connecting to Kathmandu. Upon arrival in Kathmandu, you can rest at your hotel or explore the city's many attractions. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner. Our representative will transfer you to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of your journey to the Last Forbidden Kingdom.
