A Spiritual Journey Through the Hidden Valley

The Tsum Valley Trek is one of Nepal's most extraordinary and sacred journeys, taking you through a hidden Himalayan valley that remained isolated from the outside world until 2008. Known as the "Valley of Bliss," Tsum is a pristine Buddhist sanctuary where ancient monasteries, centuries-old traditions, and breathtaking mountain scenery combine to create an unforgettable spiritual adventure.

This 19-day pilgrimage through the Tsum Valley offers a unique opportunity to explore remote Tibetan villages, visit ancient monasteries including the famous Mu Gompa and Rachen Gompa, and witness a way of life that has remained virtually unchanged for generations. The trek takes you to elevations of up to 4,850m at Nile Village, the highest settlement in the valley, where sacred caves and pilgrimage sites await.

Unlike the more crowded trekking routes in Nepal, the Tsum Valley remains relatively untouched, offering intrepid travelers an authentic Himalayan experience with warm hospitality from local communities who follow Tibetan Buddhist traditions. This is not just a trek - it's a journey into the heart of Himalayan spirituality and culture.

Detailed Itinerary

19 Days Trekking Adventure

A complete day-by-day breakdown of your Tsum Valley journey

1

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Welcome to Nepal! Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, you will be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with a traditional cultural show. Later, receive a trek briefing and prepare your equipment for the adventure ahead.

Accommodation: Hotel in Kathmandu
2

Day 2: Drive to Arughat (540m)

After an early breakfast, embark on a scenic 6-7 hour drive to Arughat, the gateway to the Manaslu region and Tsum Valley. The journey follows the Trishuli River valley, offering stunning views of terraced hillsides and rural Nepali villages.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Arughat | Drive: 6-7 hours
3

Day 3: Trek to Soti Khola (700m)

Begin your trek from Arughat to Soti Khola, following the Budhi Gandaki River. The trail passes through beautiful forests, rural villages, and terraced fields. Cross several suspension bridges and enjoy the pristine natural surroundings.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Soti Khola | Trek: 5-6 hours
4

Day 4: Acclimatization at Soti Khola

Take a rest day at Soti Khola for acclimatization and exploration. Visit local villages, interact with friendly locals, and learn about their traditional way of life. This rest day is crucial for adapting to the altitude before heading higher.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Soti Khola | Rest day
5

Day 5: Trek to Jagat (1,340m)

Continue your trek to Jagat, entering the Manaslu Conservation Area. The trail passes through beautiful forests and crosses the Budhi Gandaki River several times. Jagat is a charming village with stone houses and local temples.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Jagat | Trek: 5-6 hours
6

Day 6: Exploration at Jagat

Spend a day exploring Jagat and its surroundings. Visit the local temple, interact with the community, and soak in the cultural atmosphere. This day also helps with further acclimatization as you prepare to enter the Tsum Valley.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Jagat | Exploration day
7

Day 7: Trek to Chumling (2,386m)

Today you enter the sacred Tsum Valley. The trail climbs through beautiful forests and crosses suspension bridges as you ascend to Chumling. This picturesque village is known for its ancient monastery and traditional Tibetan architecture.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Chumling | Trek: 5-6 hours
8

Day 8: Cultural Day at Chumling

Explore Chumling and visit the historic Chumling Monastery, one of the oldest in the Tsum Valley. Witness Buddhist prayer ceremonies and interact with the local Tibetan Buddhist community. Learn about their unique traditions and spiritual practices.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Chumling | Cultural exploration
9

Day 9: Trek to Mu Gompa (3,700m)

Ascend to Mu Gompa, the largest and most important monastery in the Tsum Valley. The trail climbs through alpine terrain with stunning mountain views. Mu Gompa is home to ancient Buddhist scriptures and a community of monks.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Mu Gompa | Trek: 6-7 hours
10

Day 10: Mu Gompa Monastery Visit

Spend the day exploring Mu Gompa and its surroundings. Visit the ancient monastery complex, explore nearby caves used for meditation by Buddhist practitioners, and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere. Interact with the monks and learn about Buddhist philosophy.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Mu Gompa | Monastery exploration
11

Day 11: Nile Village (4,850m)

Trek to Nile Village, the highest settlement in the Tsum Valley at 4,850m. This remote village is a major Buddhist pilgrimage site with ancient temples and sacred caves. The views from here are spectacular, overlooking the Manaslu range.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Nile Village | Trek: 4-5 hours
12

Day 12: Rachen Gompa Visit

Visit Rachen Gompa, a beautiful nunnery located in the upper Tsum Valley. This peaceful nunnery is home to numerous nuns who dedicate their lives to Buddhist practice. Witness prayer ceremonies and learn about their daily spiritual practices.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Upper Tsum | Trek: 3-4 hours
13

Day 13: Buddhist Sites Exploration

Spend a full day exploring the sacred sites of the upper Tsum Valley. Visit ancient temples, sacred caves, and pilgrimage sites that hold deep spiritual significance for Tibetan Buddhists. Immerse yourself in the spiritual atmosphere of this sacred valley.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Upper Tsum | Full day exploration
14

Day 14: Return Trek to Chumling

Begin your return journey, retracing your steps back through the Tsum Valley to Chumling. Enjoy different perspectives of the landscape as you descend. The lower altitude makes the walking easier.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Chumling | Trek: 6-7 hours
15

Day 15: Return Trek to Jagat

Continue descending to Jagat, passing through familiar villages and forests. Take time to appreciate the natural beauty of the Manaslu Conservation Area. Arrive in Jagat and rest after the long trek.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Jagat | Trek: 6-7 hours
16

Day 16: Return Trek to Soti Khola

Continue your descent to Soti Khola, following the Budhi Gandaki River through lush forests and rural villages. The lower altitude brings warmer temperatures and easier walking. Arrive in Soti Khola and celebrate your achievement.

Accommodation: Teahouse in Soti Khola | Trek: 5-6 hours
17

Day 17: Drive to Kathmandu

After breakfast, drive back to Kathmandu (6-7 hours). The evening is free for exploration, shopping for souvenirs, or simply relaxing. Enjoy a farewell dinner with the team and share your experiences.

Accommodation: Hotel in Kathmandu | Drive: 6-7 hours
18

Day 18: Buffer Day

This contingency day is built into the itinerary to account for any weather delays or unexpected circumstances. Use this day for rest, optional sightseeing in Kathmandu, or last-minute shopping.

Accommodation: Hotel in Kathmandu | Rest day
19

Day 19: Departure

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Tribhuvan International Airport for your departure flight. Bid farewell to Nepal with unforgettable memories of your sacred Tsum Valley pilgrimage!
