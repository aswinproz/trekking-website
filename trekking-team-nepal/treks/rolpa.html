Trek Itinerary

A detailed 14-day plan to explore the remote Rolpa region.

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Welcome to Nepal! We'll pick you up from the airport and bring you to your hotel in Thamel.

Day 2: Fly to Nepalgunj

A scenic flight to the western city of Nepalgunj, near the Indian border.

Day 3-12: Trekking in Rolpa

Experience the rugged beauty of Western Nepal. Visit remote villages, cross pristine rivers, and enjoy views of the Dhaulagiri and Sisne ranges.

Day 13-14: Return & Departure

Fly back to Kathmandu for a farewell dinner before your international departure.
