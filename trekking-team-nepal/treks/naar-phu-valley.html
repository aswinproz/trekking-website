Discover the Ancient Trading Routes

The Naar Phu Valley Trek takes you on an extraordinary journey through one of Nepal's most remote and culturally rich regions. Following ancient trading routes that once connected Nepal to Tibet, this 17-day expedition explores the hidden valleys of Naar and Phu, where traditional Tibetan Buddhist culture has been preserved for centuries.

Trek through picturesque villages with stone houses and ancient monasteries, cross high mountain passes with breathtaking views of 7,000 and 8,000-meter peaks, and immerse yourself in a world that feels remarkably untouched by modern civilization. The region remained closed to foreign trekkers until 1992, ensuring an authentic experience far from the crowded trails of more famous routes.

Trek Highlights

  • Explore ancient Tibetan villages with centuries-old traditions
  • Walk along historic trading routes connecting Nepal to Tibet
  • Visit remote monasteries including Naar Monastery
  • Cross high mountain passes with panoramic Himalayan views
  • Experience authentic Buddhist culture in pristine settings
  • Trek through the dramatic Phu Valley - one of Nepal's least visited regions
  • Witness stunning views of peaks exceeding 8,000 meters
  • Discover rare wildlife in the Annapurna Conservation Area

Detailed Itinerary

17-Day Naar Phu Valley Trek

A complete journey through ancient trading routes and remote Himalayan valleys

1 Arrival in Kathmandu

+

Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with a traditional Nepali cultural show. Your trek leader will provide a detailed briefing about the upcoming adventure and help you prepare all necessary equipment and permits.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu | Meals: Dinner

2 Drive to Besishahar (760m)

+

After an early breakfast, embark on a scenic 6-7 hour drive to Besishahar, the gateway to the Annapurna region. The winding road follows the Trishuli River, offering spectacular views of terraced farmlands, traditional villages, and the Himalayan foothills. Upon arrival, settle into your lodge and meet your trekking crew.

Overnight: Teahouse in Besishahar | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

3 Trek to Ghermu (1,650m)

+

Your trek begins! Follow the marshydi river valley through charming villages with stone houses and terraced fields. The trail passes through sub-tropical forests with occasional views of the higher peaks emerging above the ridgeline. Arrive in Ghermu, a picturesque village known for its apple orchards and traditional architecture.

Overnight: Teahouse in Ghermu | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 5-6 hours

4 Trek to Ngadi (2,050m)

+

Continue ascending through the Ngadi valley, with increasingly dramatic mountain views as you gain altitude. The trail passes through dense forests of oak and pine, occasionally emerging into small settlements. Ngadi serves as the last major village before entering the restricted area, offering a glimpse into traditional mountain life.

Overnight: Teahouse in Ngadi | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 5-6 hours

5 Trek to Koto (2,600m)

+

Today's trek takes you to Koto, the entry point to the restricted Naar Phu area. After obtaining your permits at the checkpoint, continue to this small settlement nestled beneath towering cliffs. The landscape transitions from lush green valleys to more arid, dramatic terrain characteristic of the trans-Himalayan region.

Overnight: Teahouse in Koto | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 6-7 hours

6 Trek to Meta (3,560m)

+

Leaving Koto, the trail ascends dramatically into the Phu Valley. Cross several streams and climb through a narrow canyon before emerging onto the alpine plateau around Meta. This is your first major altitude milestone, and the views become increasingly spectacular as you rise above the tree line.

Overnight: Teahouse in Meta | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 6-7 hours

7 Trek to Phu (4,250m)

+

The highlight of the trek awaits as you make your way to the remote village of Phu. Perched on a dramatic hillside, Phu is one of the highest settlements in Nepal and features fascinating Tibetan-style architecture. The village is home to several ancient monasteries and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks.

Overnight: Teahouse in Phu | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 6-7 hours

8 Explore Phu Valley

+

Take a well-deserved rest day to explore the Phu Valley. Visit the historic Phu Monastery, explore the medieval village with its intricate stone carvings, or embark on a day hike toward Himlung Himal Base Camp. This is a fantastic opportunity to immerse yourself in the local culture and enjoy the pristine mountain scenery without the pressure of daily trekking.

Overnight: Teahouse in Phu | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Activity: Exploration/Hiking

9 Trek to Naar (4,100m)

+

Today's trek crosses the Naar-Phu Pass (5,300m), the highest point of the trek. The ascent is challenging but rewarded with panoramic views of peaks exceeding 8,000 meters. After crossing the pass, descend into the Naar Valley, a remote wilderness area with few settlements and pristine natural beauty.

Overnight: Teahouse in Naar | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 6-7 hours

10 Explore Naar Valley

+

Discover the ancient Naar Valley, home to the historic Naar Monastery and traditional Tibetan villages. Explore the village of Naar, visit centuries-old religious sites, and enjoy day hikes to surrounding viewpoints. The valley offers incredible opportunities for photography and cultural immersion in one of Nepal's most sacred regions.

Overnight: Teahouse in Naar | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Activity: Exploration

11 Rest and Exploration Day

+

This acclimatization day allows your body to adjust to the high altitude while providing additional time to explore the region. Take short walks around the village, interact with local families, or simply relax and enjoy the stunning mountain scenery. Proper acclimatization is crucial for a safe and enjoyable trek.

Overnight: Teahouse in Naar | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Activity: Rest/Exploration

12 Optional Extension - Tilicho Lake

+

Those seeking additional adventure can opt to trek to the spectacular Tilicho Lake (4,920m), one of the highest glacial lakes in the world. Known for its striking turquoise waters set against dramatic mountain backdrop, Tilicho is a highlight of the region. Alternatively, explore more of the Naar Valley or simply rest and prepare for the return journey.

Overnight: Teahouse in Naar or Tilicho | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Activity: Optional extension

13 Trek to Koto

+

Begin your return journey, retracing your steps through the Phu Valley back to Koto. The descent is easier than the ascent, allowing you to appreciate the landscape from a different perspective. Arrive in Koto by late afternoon and enjoy a well-deserved rest.

Overnight: Teahouse in Koto | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 6-7 hours

14 Trek to Ngadi

+

Continue descending through the valley, passing through Meta and back toward Ngadi. As you lose altitude, the landscape transforms once again, with lush vegetation returning and warmer temperatures. Reflect on your incredible journey through the remote Himalayas.

Overnight: Teahouse in Ngadi | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 5-6 hours

15 Trek to Besishahar

+

Complete your trek with the final descent to Besishahar. The trail follows the Marshyangdi River, passing through familiar villages and terraced fields. Arrive in Besishahar in the afternoon, where you can celebrate the completion of your epic adventure with your trekking crew.

Overnight: Teahouse in Besishahar | Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Trekking: 5-6 hours

16 Drive to Kathmandu

+

Return to Kathmandu by road, enjoying the scenic drive through the Trishuli valley. The rest of the day is free for relaxation or optional sightseeing in Kathmandu's many temples, markets, and cultural attractions. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner to celebrate your successful trek.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu | Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Driving: 6-7 hours

17 Departure

+

After breakfast, our representative will transfer you to Tribhuvan International Airport for your departure flight. As you leave Nepal, you carry with you unforgettable memories of the ancient trading routes, remote Himalayan villages, and pristine wilderness of the Naar Phu Valley.

Meals: Breakfast
