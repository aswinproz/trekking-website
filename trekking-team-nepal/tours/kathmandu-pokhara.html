Tour Itinerary

The perfect transition from the capital to the lake city.

Day 1: Departure from Kathmandu

Begin the drive along the Prithvi Highway. Stop at the Manakamana Temple (via cable car) and continue to the charming village of Bandipur.

Day 2: Bandipur to Pokhara

Explore the cobblestone streets of Bandipur in the morning. After lunch, drive to Pokhara and check in to your lakeside hotel.

Day 3-4: Pokhara Activities

Full days of exploration: boating, hiking, and mountain views. Optional paragliding or ultra-light flight.

Day 5: Departure

Final morning in Pokhara and transfer to your next destination.
