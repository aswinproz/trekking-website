Your Journey

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Lakeside Exploration

Arrival in Pokhara and check-in to your hotel. Afternoon walk along the vibrant Lakeside street and enjoy a welcome dinner with lake views.

Day 2: Sarangkot Sunrise & City Tour

Early morning drive to Sarangkot for sunrise. After breakfast, visit Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Cave, and the International Mountain Museum.

Day 3: Boating & Departure

Morning boating on Phewa Lake and visit to Tal Barahi Temple. Afternoon transfer to airport or bus station for departure.
