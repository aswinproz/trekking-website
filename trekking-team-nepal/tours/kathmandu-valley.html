Tour Itinerary

A comprehensive 3-day journey through the heart of Kathmandu.

Day 1: Arrival & Kathmandu Durbar Square

Arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport and transfer to hotel. Afternoon visit to Kathmandu Durbar Square, home to the Living Goddess Kumari.

Day 2: Pashupatinath, Boudhanath & Swayambhunath

A spiritual journey visiting the holy Hindu temple of Pashupatinath, the massive Boudhanath Stupa, and the hilltop Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple).

Day 3: Patan & Bhaktapur Exploration

Visit the ancient cities of Patan and Bhaktapur, famous for their exquisite woodcarvings, metalwork, and medieval architecture. Afternoon departure.
