Scenic Journey

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Nagarkot

Arrival in Kathmandu and transfer to the hilltop resort in Nagarkot. Enjoy the sunset over the Himalayas.

Day 2: Sunrise & Bhaktapur

Early sunrise views. After breakfast, visit the medieval city of Bhaktapur before returning to Kathmandu.

Day 3: Fly to Pokhara

Scenic flight to Pokhara. Afternoon boating on Phewa Lake and a short hike to World Peace Pagoda.

Day 4: Sarangkot & City Sightseeing

Drive to Sarangkot for sunrise. Later, explore Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Cave, and the Seti River Gorge.

Day 5: Australian Camp Hike

A short drive and hike to Australian Camp for a closer view of the Annapurna massif. Overnight in Pokhara.

Day 6: Return & Departure

Flight back to Kathmandu and transfer to the international airport for departure.
