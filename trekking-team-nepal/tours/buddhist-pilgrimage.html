Swayambhunath

Sacred Journey

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Boudhanath

Arrival in Kathmandu. Evening visit to Boudhanath Stupa, the center of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal.

Day 2: Kathmandu Heritage Sites

Visit Swayambhunath, Kapan Monastery, and the ancient city of Patan to see the Golden Temple.

Day 3: Flight to Lumbini

Take a short flight to Lumbini. Visit the Sacred Garden, Ashoka Pillar, and various international monasteries.

Day 4-7: Spiritual Immersion

Meditation sessions, visits to Tilaurakot (ancient Kapilvastu), and surrounding Buddhist archaeological sites.

Day 8: Departure

Morning flight back to Kathmandu and onward departure.
