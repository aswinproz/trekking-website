Our Story

Your Trusted Himalayan Partner Since 2005

Trekking Team Nepal was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate mountaineers and adventure enthusiasts who shared a dream: to share the magic of the Himalayas with the world while supporting local communities.

What started as a small operation with just a handful of guides has grown into one of Nepal's most trusted trekking companies. Over the years, we've helped over 5,000 adventurers from around the world experience the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas.

Our team consists of certified Sherpa guides, many of whom have summited Mount Everest multiple times, along with experienced support staff, porters, and cooks who work together to ensure every trekker has an unforgettable experience.
