Discover the Wild Heart of Nepal

Embark on an unforgettable 3-day jungle safari adventure in Chitwan National Park, Nepal's first and most celebrated national park. Designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Chitwan offers an incredible diversity of flora and fauna in its tropical and subtropical landscapes.

Your adventure includes exciting jeep safaris deep into the jungle, peaceful canoe rides along the Rapti River, thrilling elephant bathing experiences, and immersive visits to Tharu villages. This is your chance to witness the majestic one-horned rhinoceros in the wild, search for the elusive Bengal tiger, and discover over 500 species of birds.

Whether you are a wildlife enthusiast, nature photographer, or simply seeking a break from trekking, Chitwan Jungle Safari provides the perfect blend of adventure, culture, and natural beauty.

Your Adventure

Detailed Itinerary

1 Drive to Chitwan - Elephant Briefing - Village Walk

+

Your exciting jungle safari adventure begins with an early morning departure from Kathmandu. Enjoy a scenic 5-6 hour drive through the Nepalese countryside.

Upon arrival at your jungle lodge in Chitwan, you will be greeted with a refreshing welcome drink and shown to your comfortable room. After settling in and enjoying a delicious lunch, gather for an important elephant briefing session.

In the late afternoon, take a leisurely walk through the nearby Tharu village. In the evening, enjoy a captivating traditional Tharu dance performance.

2 Full Day Jungle Activities

+

Wake up early for the most exciting day! After early morning tea, depart for a thrilling jeep safari into the depths of Chitwan National Park.

Search for the magnificent one-horned rhinoceros and keep your eyes peeled for the elusive Bengal tiger, wild elephants, sambar deer, and various monkey species.

After lunch, head out for a peaceful canoe ride along the Rapti River. The highlight is the unique elephant bathing experience. End the day with a spectacular sunset view from the riverbank.

3 Morning Bird Watching - Return to Kathmandu

+

For bird enthusiasts, this morning offers a special treat. Join your naturalist guide for an early morning bird watching excursion.

Chitwan National Park is home to over 500 species of birds. Look out for the majestic Bengal florican, the endangered great hornbill, various kingfishers, and more.

After lunch, bid farewell to the jungle and begin your journey back to Kathmandu. Arrive in Kathmandu by evening with unforgettable memories!
